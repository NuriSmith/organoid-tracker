Data — Export / Import / Share

Your data lives in this browser only. To move it to another computer, share with a labmate, or back it up:

  1. Export JSON — downloads a file with all your differentiations, collections, and settings.
  2. Send that file to your labmate (email, Slack, shared drive).
  3. They open the tracker, go to Settings → Import JSON, and select the file.
  4. Their tracker now has your data. Their own data is replaced — so they should export theirs first if they have any.

To only share your cell lines library (not all your experiments), use the Export Lines JSON button above instead.
